About Kirui Pizza Place

This is an online Pizza place that assist kenyanc customers to be served their favorite cuisine, pizza, within the city of Nairobi. The customers or clients have to place orders at the company's website and then wait for delivery at the comfort of their living room. Thus, customers do not have to worry about travelling to the place and place their orders manually. However, the customers are necessited to pay an extra Ksh. 200 for the delivery to be made to their homes.
